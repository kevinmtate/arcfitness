Hi, my name is Alyssa Carrubba and I am a fitness instructor, movement coach, and music enthusiast. By pairing compelling music with dynamic movement, I create an energetic atmosphere that allows students to feel powerful.

I am forming a community of those seeking connection, motivation and purpose. Through vigorous exercises performed to the beat of music, students feel strong and better connected to themselves and those around them.

My class promotes full-body strength training, muscle toning, and cardio health. I make students feel seen through my commitment to developing collective strength and fostering connection and community united through music and movement.
